BIO

I love working in teams to find creative solutions to interesting problems. I am always open to learning new frameworks and languages and adapting to what is needed for any context. My background is in full stack software development using Java Script, React, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Active Record, HTML, and CSS. I have over 20 years of experience working on deliverables for creative teams and value good professional communication. I write clear, detailed proposals and assimilate feedback from fellow team members well. I am good at planning and creating proof-of-concepts for client approval and value outside perspectives on my solutions. If you have an idea for a project, get in touch and let's talk about how to realize your idea. Thanks for visiting!
